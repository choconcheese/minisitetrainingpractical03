Our History

Training Zone have been the leader in all kinds of training that covers management and IT in Plongk St., Cairnsville for the last 150 years.

The founder of the company, Terrence Zonas is still CEO and spokesperson for Training Zone.

Our Trainers:

Terrence Zonas

An American computer scientist and Internet entrepreneur. Co-founder of Google. His real passion and work is the Training Zone where he likes to teach people things that make a difference.



Stafford Gray

- A senior Information Technology trainer. His primary research area is Society-Oriented and/or Threshold Cryptography. His current research themes are: Multi-Party Computations, Oblivious Transfers and Secret Sharing Schemes. He also teaches a wide range of topics in Computer Science.



Adrienne Michowski

- An English mathematician and writer, chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. Her notes on the engine include what is recognised as the first algorithm intended to be carried out by a machine. Because of this, she is often regarded as the first computer programmer. She trains clients in programming and longevity.
