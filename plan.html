Mission Statement

The proposed website is intended to maximize the company’s visibility across the web by promoting the services offered by the company. With the increase in web traffic, it gives the company to attract old and new customers at the same time boosting the average profitability of each customer and the earning potential of the company’s website.

Success Evaluation

The website must be scalable and should be able to handle any amount of traffic. Hence, trackable components must be implemented.

Call-to-actions will be included at the end of the (company’s) services landing page. The company will be able to track which content is actually driving visitors to avail of the company’s services. The number of customers will be compared to the previous months whilst the downtime of the website can be evaluated during or within the 3-month period.

Target Audience

The website’s target audience would be local working adults from ages 28-50 who are either: employed, unemployed and those who are still in the job search.

The site is also aimed for those people who have free time and who would like to improve and do some advancement in their skills.
